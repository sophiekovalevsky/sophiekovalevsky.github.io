Who I am?

My name is Kiara Navarro, most commonly known as sophiekovalevsky at 3w. A couple of years ago I got my degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.

Since my departure from university, I've been dabbling in the embedded realm with a laser-focus on Internet of Things and how this industry relates to wireless communications.

My main activities gravites toward developing consumer electronics devices with some connectivity to Internet throughout signals deployed over the electromagnetic spectrum.

I'm appasionate about open source and hardware systems. I enjoy the feeling of pealing layer by layer until get to the point where there is no more to inspect that only charge in movement throughout a track within a printed circuit board.
The conventional skill profile that a conventional electronic engineer should acquires does not fit to me. I would rather consider my ability bagpack ranging from what is required to design a printed circuit board (hardware knowledge) and develop a decent firmware stack to flash into a physical device. (software background).
That's the magic in diving within IoT applications, is the fact that hardware and software worlds are (git) merged. There isn't crystal clear distintion between these two disciplines and it's way obvious nowadays how important is for an engineer to be knowledgeable in both niches.
